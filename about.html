Why Is This NGO Necessary?

It is necessary because it enables citizens to work together voluntarily to promote values which are important to them and the environment. They improve local initiative and problem solving through their activities in abroad role of fields(say what Ahsan Foundation focuses on).

What Is The Basic Course Behind The NGO?

To create awareness to the public i.e to educate, enlighten and to improve the standard of living of the less privileged be it a child or an adult. To help in good cost in different places(Graveyard, Hospitals and Mosques) to mention among the few.

How It Benefits The Society?

It benefits the society in different ways i.e

  • It reduces the rate at which children will roam about the streets to ask for help and also the rate at which children will grow up with bad habits because the foundation will educate the children at their early stage.
