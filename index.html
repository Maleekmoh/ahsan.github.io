THE CONSTITUTION OF AHSAN FOUNDATION

PREAMBLE

We the members of AHSAN FOUNDATION a non-governmental and non-profit organization do firmly and solemnly resolve to provide for ourselves a constitution and to be governed by the previous therein contained.

ARTICLE 1: NAME

The name of the association shall be "AHSAN FOUNDATION"

ARTICLE 2: ADDRESS

Her Ofice(Secretariat) shall be at a location recommended by the Executive Council and ratified by the General House.

ARTICLE 3: MOTTO

The Motto of the association shall be "Live,Laugh and Love"

ARTICLE 4: MISSION

  • To help the less privilege in the pursyuit of happiness by bringing smile on their amiable faces.
  • To provide ministration to the less privilege and to physically disabled.
  • To serve as an intermediary between donors and the needy.

ARTICLE 5: AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

AHSAN FOUNDATION shall be a non-political, non-tribal and whose objective is to;

  • Help improve the standard of living of less privileged through building optimism in them, research development and skill acquisition.
  • To provide an assembly for discussion of issues which will foster unity, progress and respect among members for moral understanding and to ensure good image, peace and unity.
  • To pay visitations and donations to hospitals, orphanage homes and prison yards which will be in specific month and date.
  • Donations to Mosques and Graveyards

ARTICLE 6: SOURCE(S) OF FUNDS

  1. Monthly donations by members
  2. Solicitation of funds from people outside the foundation

ARTICLE 7: MEMBERSHIP OF ASSOCIATION

Any interested person shall not be deprived of membership because of race or ethnic group.

To this every member shall

  1. Strictly abide by the constitution
  2. Attend meetings at all times unless with concrete reasons.
  3. Not be allowed to leave meetings without reasonable excuse(s) until meeting is declared close.
  4. Show commitment to attendance at activities of the foundation.
  5. Everything of the platform must be transparent.

ARTICLE 8: PROCEDURE FOR MEMBERSHIP REGISTRATION

  1. Monthly donations by members
  2. Funds solicitation from other people

ARTICLE 9: OFFICES OF THE FOUNDATION

The offices of the foundation shall be procedure for membership registrations.

  1. President
  2. Vice President
  3. Secretary
  4. Treasurer
  5. Financial Secretary
  6. Public Relation Officer
  7. Chief Distribution/Welfare
  8. Editor

ARTICLE 10: TENURE

The offices shall be elected for (2)years of Annual General Meeting and shall be eligible for a second term but must be expected to have been committed.

ARTICLE 11: AMENDMENT OF THE CONSTITUTION

The foundation may alter the provision of its constitution at a General Meeting by a resolution passed by a simple majority of its member and approved by the commission.
